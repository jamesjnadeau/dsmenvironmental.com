Our Services

Sustainability & Pollution Prevention

DSM can help businesses and manufacturers reduce their environmental impact and increase their bottom line through sustainability and pollution prevention assistance.

Pollution Prevention Assessments
DSM evaluates municipal, industrial, and commercial operations to analyze their economic and technical performance and to identify options for reducing resource use and generation of hazardous and solid waste.

Sustainable Solutions
DSM has a long history aimed at helping private sector clients better meet the challenges of improving the triple bottom line of financial profit, environmental performance, and social responsibility. DSM works with companies to:

  • Provide environmental performance benchmarks;
  • Develop or provide third party review for environmental and corporate social responsibility (CSR) reports;
  • Leverage existing resources or add new systems to improve financial, environmental and social performance; and,
  • Quantify and benchmark greenhouse gas emissions throughout operations to set goals for reducing the facility or organization's carbon footprint.

Climate Change Impact Reduction
DSM has been working diligently to quantify greenhouse gas emissions resulting from waste management to help local, county and state governments incorporate cradle to grave carbon impacts from recycling, composting and resource recovery management programs.
